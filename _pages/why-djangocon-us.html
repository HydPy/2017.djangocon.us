Benefits of DjangoCon US

Attending DjangoCon US benefits your organization as well as your employees:

  • Tutorials provide cost-effective, hands-on training
  • Sessions cover a wide variety of Django topics
  • Attendees can network with maintainers of popular libraries
  • Being exposed to new technologies and ideas reinvigorates an employee’s passion for development
  • Employees can share what they learned when they return to the office

Other Useful Topics

We have talks on denormalized query engines, files, the User model, password management, spreadsheets, learning Django, React, and so much more. There is something for everyone at DjangoCon US!

Sprints

Sprints are happening during two completely free days directly after the main conference. Programmers work intensively together on special projects, with the aid of other experts in the community. They are also great for learning and trading skills.

DjangoCon provides a great opportunity for Django enthusiasts and experts to meet, network, and share experiences and best practices. The quantity of training sessions mixed with case studies and networking opportunities provides a huge ROI for employers who want to help employees improve their technical chops quickly and efficiently.
